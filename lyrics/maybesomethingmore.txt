He calls his magic "science," but it's all the same,
and this is what the two were taught for several years,
the nature of the strangest stones and strongest flame,
of seas, disease, eternal freeze, and golden gears.
And as the secret skills of magic were revealed,
they grew together, ever closer, unaware
of how the other felt, until their fates were sealed,
and knew one day that maybe something more was there.
