With endless gold, triumphantly, the men returned,
and carried back the sickness in their blood and breath.
In Edilar, the ashen sky and soil burned,
but life will always overcome the greatest death.
Their ship had drifted slowly in the ocean foam,
until it struck the rocky shore of solid earth.
A lost and ancient temple soon became their home,
and by the dawn of spring, the elf had given birth.
