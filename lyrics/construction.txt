While she excelled in sickness spells, and blood and bones,
he sought the mystic power of the smallest specks,
but by the time her new and vicious plague had grown,
he'd drafted up a craft he knew was too complex.
But no, the Council dedicated everything
to building bombs exactly as the elf had planned.
A pair of pearls, he fashioned into glowing rings
to find the weapons, should they fall to other hands.
